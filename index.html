D DECLARE - Critical Assumptions
List what MUST be true for success: technology capabilities, market acceptance, regulatory environment, organizational readiness, competitive response
Template:
Critical assumptions: AI accuracy reaches [X]%, regulatory approval for [specific process], customer acceptance of [AI application], staff successfully retrain for AI collaboration, data quality improves to support AI, competitors don't block innovation through [specific concern]
E EXPLAIN - Analysis Needed
What specific help do you need to validate this goal and identify the pathway from today to breakthrough success?
Template:
Need analysis of: technical feasibility gaps, implementation timeline with milestones, change management requirements, competitive advantage sustainability, risk mitigation strategies, resource requirements, regulatory considerations, market readiness indicators.
R REQUEST - Innovation Roadmap
Ask for specific deliverables that will help you move from goal to business opportunities with timelines and success metrics
Template:
Please provide: (1) 18-month implementation roadmap with quarterly milestones, (2) Risk assessment with mitigation strategies, (3) Success metrics and validation checkpoints, (4) Required capabilities and partnerships, (5) Competitive advantage analysis, (6) Business model implications and opportunities
